## When shortcuts do not work

For os x terminal:

Press <kbd>⌘ command</kbd>+<kbd>⌥ option</kbd>+<kbd>o</kbd>, you will see 'Option is Meta'.

Now work:

- <kbd>⌘ command</kbd>+<kbd>s</kbd>
- <kbd>⌘ command</kbd>+<kbd>o</kbd>
- <kbd>⌘ command</kbd>+<kbd>i</kbd>
