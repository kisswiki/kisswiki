## Filter

Look for any unread:

`from:allegro.pl is:unread`


Search for those already archived:

`from:allegro.pl -in:Sent -in:Chat -in:Draft -in:Inbox`

Only read:

`from:allegro.pl is:read -in:Sent -in:Chat -in:Draft -in:Inbox`
