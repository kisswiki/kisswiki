MITTZON

Poke the pinhole at the bottom of the hand controller with a pin to enter the reset status (at this time, the orange light will be steady on)

Long press the down button until the table goes down to the lowest height

Raise it up 4 mm automatically to complete the reset.

After the reset is successful, the orange light will turn off.
