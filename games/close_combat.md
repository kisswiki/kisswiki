### Shortcuts

- CLICK+DRAG - move
- CTRL+U
- CTRL+
- F5
- F6
- F7
- F9
- SPACE - command radius
- SHIFT+CLICK
- mouse wheel - cycle in squads
- Z - Move
- X - Move Fast
- C - Sneak
- V - Fire
- B - Smoke
- N - Defend (you can click to set direction)
- M - Ambush
- , - Dismount (the comma key)
